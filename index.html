About us

DOM KOM is a modern European company specializing in the manufacture of containers for modular housing and offices. We use only quality materials and reliable technologies.

The sandwich panel container is the perfect solution if you need:

  • comfortable office for engineers and designers on the construction site;
  • accommodation and auxiliary space for production workers;
  • retail outlet in close proximity to the production site;
  • temporary housing for refugees or travellers;
  • reliable security post or convenient warehouse.

Block containers have many advantages. They can be installed quickly and, if necessary, dismantled and moved to a new location so that your business does not stop for a moment.

Heat and noise insulation, electricity supply, convector and / or air conditioning allow you to live or work comfortably in any weather conditions. There is enough space for 3-4 people in one container. Block containers can be combined into two-story modules, so you get extra square meters when you need to quickly scale your project.

In this way, you will be able to ensure a long and comfortable stay of workers in a convenient location for you and them, regardless of the climate.
